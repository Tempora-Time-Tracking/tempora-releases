Tempora 0.4.5

  • New — Show today's logged time in the menu bar. Turn it on in Settings › General › Menu bar to keep your running total for the day in view, alongside the timer.
  • New — Choose where a Teams call's title goes. Under Settings › Tracking › Teams call you can put the meeting name in the entry's notes (the default) or its title.
  • Improved — When a call ends and the entry editor opens on its own, Tempora now plays the notification sound so you don't miss it.
  • Improved — Various technical improvements under the hood.
