Tempora 0.1

The first release of Tempora — a native macOS time tracker that turns the hours you actually work into clean, organized records, with almost no manual bookkeeping.

  • Timers — start, pause, and stop from the toolbar, menu bar, or keyboard; schedule timers for later and let them start automatically.
  • Automatic activity tracking — records the apps and windows you use (including browser tabs), with idle detection and working-hours limits so breaks don't pad your hours.
  • Visual timeline — your day color-coded by project, with zoom, drag-to-select, and your calendar events shown alongside.
  • Jira worklogs — one-click batch upload of entries to their tickets, with smart reminders when unlogged work piles up.
  • Insights & portability — per-project stats and charts, JSON/CSV export, JSON import.
  • Automation — Spotlight, Siri & Shortcuts actions, AppleScript, and a URL scheme.

Your data stays on your Mac; Jira credentials live in the Keychain. Thanks for trying Tempora — feedback is always welcome.
