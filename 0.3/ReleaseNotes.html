Tempora 0.3

  • Fixed — Calendar integration now works in the installed app. Turning it on in Settings → Calendar prompts for access as expected, your calendars appear, and events show in a dedicated lane on the day timeline where you can turn a meeting into a time entry with a click. (Previously the permission prompt never appeared, so no calendars or events were shown.)
  • New — Settings options that depend on another option now clearly show as off until that option is enabled, so an unavailable toggle no longer looks active.
  • Fixed — Microsoft Teams call detection no longer names an entry "Sharing control bar" when you share your screen during a call.
  • Fixed — after an unexpected quit, an in-progress app-usage segment is recovered up to its last recorded activity instead of being stretched to the time you reopened Tempora.
