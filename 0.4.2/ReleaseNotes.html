Tempora 0.4.2

  • Changed — Timer presets are now called tickets. The name fits what they had become: a saved title and project you track work against, not just a way to start a timer. Your existing presets carry over as tickets — nothing to redo.
  • New — The menu-bar timer popover now shows how much you have logged today, so you can check the day's total without opening Tempora.
  • New — Tickets appear as suggestions while you type an entry or timer title, so you can fill in a ticket id and project in one step.
  • Improved — When a call ends and Jira is set up, the meeting name now goes into the notes field and the title is left free — pick a ticket there to fill in its id and project, and the meeting name still travels to Jira as the worklog comment.
  • Improved — New tickets start with "prepend title" turned off.
